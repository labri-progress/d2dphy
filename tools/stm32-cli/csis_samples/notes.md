# RSSI gathering

2 runs of PHYsec keygen between Alice and Bob.

- **static run:** Bob is located in an appartment in the city (floor 4.5) and Alice is located in the garden. They are approximately 25-30 meters apart.
- **mobile run:** Bob is located in an appartment in the city (floor 4.5) and Alice is moving in the garden. They are approximately 15-40 meters apart.

Then run with 1500 samples involves Alice and Bob being very close (3 meters), separated by walls, in an appartment in the city (floor 4.5).
